The Violet Society

A startup sisterhood for future founders.

About The Violet Society

Young women who join The Violet Society will have access to a group of diverse individuals who will work together to make their ideas into realities. Through the organization, members will learn about tech events, hackathons, social gatherings, and educational workshops.



Why Violet?

The Violet Society is named after Violet Baudelaire from A Series of Unfortunate Events. Like the character, members are inventive, creative, and resourceful problem-solvers. Members are encouraged to create things and share them with the community in a supportive and helpful environment.

Contact The Violet Society

Please connect with us through social media channels to stay up to date with events, opportunities, and discussions. Young women who are interested in joining should request to join the Facebook group! It's the easiest way to connect to the rest of the organization.
