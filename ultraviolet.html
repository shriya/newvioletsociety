ULTRAVIOLET

The mentor subgroup of The Violet Society

UltraViolet is critical for Violet Society members' success since it's made of female & non-binary founders who are a step ahead of them. You'll form bonds with one another through invite-only mastermind dinners once a month. You'll also meet the members of each fellowship cohort of The Violet Society at curated happy hours to help them through the ups and downs of starting companies.


SUBSCRIBE TO STAY UP TO DATE

WHAT STAGE SHOULD THESE STARTUPS BE AT?

UltraViolet is for founders who have early traction. You may be...

POST-FUNDING that you've raised from institutional investors as a Pre-Seed, Seed, or Series A round.
POST-REVENUE that can support at least 1 full-time founder.
POST-ACCELERATOR from a program that involved an investment.

We welcome founders at these different stages. If you're beyond them, we would love to invite you as a featured guest or speaker!
