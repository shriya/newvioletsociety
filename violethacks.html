About The Violet Society

Violet Hacks is run by The Violet Society, a startup sisterhood for future founders. We run high-touch, face-to-face programs for ambitious women and non-binary people who are aspiring and early-stage founders. The Violet Society gives members the confidence, network, and skills they need to launch a company.


FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is the event free?

No; your ticket includes connections to dozens of companies, 10+ founders and investors, 5 meals plus snacks, 4-6 workshops, and potential for prizes worth hundreds of dollars!


When is it? Can I stay overnight?

The event will be Saturday, Nov 3 from 10am-10pm at Domino Data Lab.


Can I come if I'm in high school?

This is an 18+ event, no minors will be allowed, sorry!


Do you offer travel reimbursement?

No travel reimbursement is available for this event.


What are the terms of participation?

Terms of Participation can be seen here. In non-legalese, it means everything that you make at this event is your property to keep working on after the event, but we are allowed to share materials you create over the weekend for promotional purposes.
