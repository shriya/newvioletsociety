About The Violet Society

The Violet Society is a startup sisterhood for future founders.

We run high-touch, face-to-face programs for ambitious women in the first 10 years of their career who are aspiring and early-stage founders. The Violet Society gives members the confidence, network, and skills they need to launch a company.
